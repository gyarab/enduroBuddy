2. What personal data we process

We process the following categories of personal data:

Registration and profile data

  • first name and last name
  • email address
  • role in the application, coach or athlete
  • login credentials, with passwords stored in hashed form using Django PBKDF2
  • optionally, Google profile data when signing in with Google

Training and performance data

  • planned sessions, including date, title, distance, notes, and two-phase days
  • completed sessions, including time, distance, average heart rate, subjective feeling, and notes
  • imported activity data from FIT files or Garmin, including start time, duration, distance, average and maximum heart rate, pace, cadence, power, elevation, interval data, and time series
  • metadata of imported files, including original filename and SHA-256 checksum

Note regarding GPS data: the application currently stores activity time series without persistent GPS coordinates. FIT files may technically contain GPS data; these data are processed temporarily in server memory during import and are not stored permanently. A technical audit has confirmed that GPS coordinates are not written to the database. If this behavior changes in the future, this policy will be updated accordingly and the relevant legal basis will be ensured.

Relationship and organizational data

  • coach-athlete links, groups, and invitations
  • email address entered when inviting another user

Garmin Connect integration, optional

  • Garmin email and displayed name
  • encrypted authentication token for access to Garmin Connect
  • the Garmin Connect password is used only during initial linking and is not stored permanently

4. Recipients of personal data and transfers to third parties

Your personal data may be transferred to the following third parties, only to the extent necessary for operating the service:

  • Hetzner Online GmbH, hosting provider
    Server infrastructure is located in Germany within the EU. Processing takes place under a data processing agreement compliant with GDPR.
  • Google LLC, sign-in with Google
    If you use sign-in with Google, basic profile data are transferred through OAuth 2.0. This processing is subject to Google's own privacy policy.
  • Resend Inc., email service provider
    Transactional emails such as invitations and notifications are sent through Resend. Only the recipient's email address and message content are transferred.
  • Garmin Ltd., Garmin Connect integration
    When optional Garmin Connect integration is used, communication with Garmin Connect APIs takes place. This processing is subject to Garmin's own terms and privacy rules.

We do not sell personal data and do not transfer them to third parties for marketing or profiling purposes.

Transfers of personal data to the United States take place on the basis of Standard Contractual Clauses approved by the European Commission. Participation of the respective companies in the EU-US Data Privacy Framework is continuously verified on dataprivacyframework.gov, and the currently recorded status is as follows:

  • Google LLC - participant in the EU-US Data Privacy Framework
  • Resend Inc. - participant in the EU-US Data Privacy Framework and the UK Extension to the EU-US DPF, verified on April 4, 2026

Hosting infrastructure operated by Hetzner is located exclusively within the EU, and no transfer outside the EEA takes place in that part of the service.

6. Cookies and technical means

The application uses only technically necessary cookies:

  • session cookie used to keep the user signed in during the visit
  • CSRF token used to protect against Cross-Site Request Forgery attacks

These cookies are necessary for the functioning of the service. We do not use any analytics, marketing, or tracking cookies, nor any tools for monitoring user behavior such as Google Analytics or Meta Pixel.

7. Minors

The service may be used by persons under 18 years of age. For users under 15 years of age, parental or guardian consent is required. This threshold follows from Article 8 GDPR in conjunction with Section 7(3) of Czech Act No. 110/2019 Coll., which lowers the age for independent consent from 16 to 15 in the Czech Republic.

If you are a legal guardian and learn that your child created an account without your knowledge, please contact us at support@endurobuddy.cz and we will delete the account without undue delay.

10. Security of personal data

We implement technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data, in particular:

  • data transmission is encrypted using TLS and HTTPS, terminated by Traefik
  • passwords are stored in hashed form using Django PBKDF2
  • Garmin authentication tokens are stored in encrypted form
  • access to data is restricted by roles, and coaches can only see the data of their own athletes
  • server infrastructure is operated in a secure Hetzner data center within the EU

11. Changes to this policy

We will inform you of material changes to this policy by email or by notice in the application at least 30 days before such changes take effect. The current version is always available on the Privacy Policy page within the application.
