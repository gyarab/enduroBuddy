Privacy Policy

EnduroBuddy - effective from April 1, 2026

1. Controller of personal data

The controller of personal data is:
Name: Vojtěch Holcman
Address: K lánu 496/2, 160 00 Praha 6, Czech Republic
Email: support@endurobuddy.cz

2. What personal data we process

We process the following categories of personal data:

Registration and profile data

  • first name and last name
  • email address
  • role in the application, coach or athlete
  • login credentials, including a securely protected password
  • optionally, Google profile data when signing in with Google

Training and performance data

  • planned sessions, including date, title, distance, notes, and two-phase days
  • completed sessions, including time, distance, average heart rate, subjective feeling, and notes
  • imported activity data from FIT files or Garmin, including start time, duration, distance, average and maximum heart rate, pace, cadence, power, elevation, interval data, and time series
  • metadata of imported files, including the original filename

Note regarding GPS data: the application does not currently store location data as part of standard activity records. If this changes in the future, this policy will be updated accordingly.

Relationship and organizational data

  • coach-athlete links, groups, and invitations
  • email address entered when inviting another user

Garmin Connect integration, optional

  • Garmin email and displayed name
  • securely stored credentials needed for Garmin Connect connection
  • the Garmin Connect password is used only during initial linking and is not stored permanently

3. Purpose and legal basis of processing

Purpose Legal basis under GDPR
Provision of the service, including training plan management and coach-athlete communication Performance of a contract under Article 6(1)(b)
Connection to Garmin Connect Consent under Article 6(1)(a)
Sending transactional emails, including invitations and notifications Performance of a contract under Article 6(1)(b)
Ensuring service security and reliable operation Legitimate interest under Article 6(1)(f)

Automated decision-making and profiling: the application does not carry out automated decision-making or profiling within the meaning of Article 22 GDPR. No decisions with legal or similarly significant effects are made solely on the basis of automated processing.

4. Recipients of personal data and transfers to third parties

Your personal data may be transferred to the following third parties, only to the extent necessary for operating the service:

  • Hosting and infrastructure providers
    We use service providers located within the EU to operate the application and store data securely.
  • Google sign-in provider
    If you use sign-in with Google, basic profile data needed to complete sign-in may be shared with that provider.
  • Email delivery provider
    Transactional emails such as invitations and notifications are sent through an external provider. Only data necessary for delivery are shared.
  • Garmin Connect provider
    If you choose to connect Garmin Connect, data needed for this connection are exchanged with that provider under its own terms and privacy rules.

We do not sell personal data and do not transfer them to third parties for marketing or profiling purposes.

5. Transfers of data outside the EEA

Some of our service providers may be based outside the European Economic Area or may process data there. In such cases, we use appropriate safeguards required by applicable law to protect personal data.

Where possible, the core operation of the service is provided through infrastructure located within the EU.

6. Cookies

The application uses only technically necessary cookies:

  • cookies used to keep the user signed in during the visit
  • cookies used to keep the application secure and functioning properly

These cookies are necessary for the functioning of the service. We do not use any analytics, marketing, or tracking cookies, nor any tools for monitoring user behavior such as Google Analytics or Meta Pixel.

7. Minors

The service may be used by persons under 18 years of age. For users under 15 years of age, parental or guardian consent is required. This threshold follows from Article 8 GDPR in conjunction with Section 7(3) of Czech Act No. 110/2019 Coll., which lowers the age for independent consent from 16 to 15 in the Czech Republic.

If you are a legal guardian and learn that your child created an account without your knowledge, please contact us at support@endurobuddy.cz and we will delete the account without undue delay.

8. Data retention period

Data category Retention period
Registration and profile data For the duration of the account, then up to 30 days after account deletion
Training and performance data For the duration of the account, then up to 30 days after account deletion
Data needed for Garmin Connect connection Until the integration is disconnected or the account is deleted
Operational and security logs Up to 90 days

9. Your rights

As a data subject, you have the following rights:

  • right of access under Article 15 GDPR
  • right to rectification under Article 16 GDPR
  • right to erasure under Article 17 GDPR
  • right to restriction of processing under Article 18 GDPR
  • right to data portability under Article 20 GDPR
  • right to object under Article 21 GDPR where processing is based on legitimate interest
  • right to withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent, without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing

You may exercise your rights by email at support@endurobuddy.cz. Requests will be handled without undue delay and no later than one month after receipt. In exceptional cases, due to complexity or the number of requests, this period may be extended by an additional two months, and you will be informed of such extension.

If you believe that the processing of your personal data violates GDPR, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the supervisory authority:
Úřad pro ochranu osobních údajů, Czech Republic
Pplk. Sochora 27, 170 00 Praha 7
www.uoou.cz

10. Security of personal data

We implement technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data, in particular:

  • data transmission and storage are protected by appropriate security measures, including encryption
  • passwords and access credentials are securely protected
  • data used for connected services are securely protected
  • access to data is restricted by roles, and coaches can only see the data of their own athletes
  • the service is operated on secure infrastructure within the EU

11. Changes to this policy

We will inform you of material changes to this policy by email or by notice in the application at least 30 days before such changes take effect. The current version is always available on the Privacy Policy page within the application.
